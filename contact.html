Frequently Asked Questions

What is the capacity of your largest venue?

Our Utsav & Udyaan Garden can accommodate up to 3,000 guests, ideal for large-scale events.

Do you provide catering services?

Yes, we offer full-service catering with customisable menus featuring local and international cuisine.

Is parking available?

We provide free parking for 500+ vehicles with 24/7 security throughout the event.

What technical support do you offer?

State-of-the-art AV equipment, sound systems, projectors, and dedicated technical support teams.

How far in advance should I book?

We recommend 2–3 months for major events, though we can accommodate last-minute requests.

Do you offer event planning services?

Absolutely — our professional planning team can manage every aspect of your event from concept to close.
