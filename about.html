About Adyar Garden

A legacy of excellence in Mangalore's event landscape

Our Story

Where Every
Moment Matters

Adyar Garden stands as a beacon of excellence in event management and hospitality across Mangalore. Established with a vision to transform the region into a world-class events destination, we have successfully hosted hundreds of prestigious occasions — from grand weddings to corporate summits.

Our state-of-the-art facility combines timeless architecture with modern technology, creating an environment that inspires creativity, fosters meaningful connections, and makes every event unforgettable.

Situated along NH-75 in Adyar, we are easily accessible for guests from across the city and beyond — with ample parking and seamless infrastructure designed around your comfort.
